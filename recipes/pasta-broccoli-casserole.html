Pasta-Broccoli-Casserole

Casseroles

Baked dishes have existed for thousands of years. Early casserole recipes consisted of rice that was pounded, pressed, and filled with a savoury mixture of meats such as chicken or sweetbread. Sometime around the 1870's this sense of casserole seems to have taken its current sense. Cooking in earthenware containers has always been common in most cultures, but the idea of casserole cooking as a one-dish meal became popular in the United States in the twentieth century, especially in the 1950's when new forms of lightweight metal and glass cookware appeared on the market. By the 1970's casseroles took on a less-than-sophisticated image.

Our broccoli pasta casserole with ham goes in the oven in just 25 minutes. Topped with cream sauce and cheese, it's so deliciously creamy and crunchy. The whole family will enjoy it!

Ingredients for 4 servings

  • 1 broccoli (about 500 g)
  • 1/2 tea spoon salt
  • 400 g pasta
  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 100 g ham
  • 30 g butter
  • 30 g flour
  • vegetable broth
  • 200 ml cream
  • grated Gouda cheese
  • some pepper
  • some nutmeg

Also:

  • water for cooking
  • some butter for the mold

Preparation of the casserole

  1. Prepare the Broccoli:

    Wash broccoli, drain, cut florets from stalk. Cook florets in boiling salted water for 1-2 minutes, drain and rinse with cold water. Cook pasta in salted water according to package directions, drain and rinse.

  2. Preheat Oven and Shred Ham

    Preheat oven to 180 degrees (convection oven 160 degrees) and grease a baking dish (Ø 26 cm) with a little butter. Peel and finely dice the onion and garlic. Cut ham into fine strips.

  3. Preparation of the Sauce

    Melt butter in a pot. Sauté onion and garlic cubes for 2-3 minutes, add flour and briefly sweat until light.

    Deglaze roux with cold stock, whisk until smooth and simmer for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly. Stir in cream and 1/3 of the gouda and season to taste with salt, pepper and nutmeg.

  4. And off into the oven!

    Mix broccoli, pasta and ham strips and spread in the baking dish. Pour sauce over casserole, sprinkle with remaining Gouda and bake in preheated oven for about 25 minutes until golden brown. Divide the casserole among plates. Serve with a colorful salad.
